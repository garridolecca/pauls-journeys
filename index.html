About This Map

This interactive atlas traces the four missionary journeys of the Apostle Paul as recorded in the book of Acts.

Journey Legend

First Journey (AD 46-48) — Acts 13-14
Second Journey (AD 49-52) — Acts 15:36-18:22
Third Journey (AD 53-57) — Acts 18:23-21:17
Journey to Rome (AD 59-60) — Acts 27-28

Features

  • Tap any city to see biblical events & scripture
  • Use journey chips to filter by trip
  • Toggle biblical/modern city names
  • Filter by scripture chapter or verse
  • See how each city connects to the biblical narrative
